2017 Winter Soup Club

This winter, only the freshest ingredients focused around our local landscape go into our collection. Chef Lindsay Steinberg McGrail has created eight new seasonal recipes to warm your body and delight your senses. Join the Green Zebra Winter Soup Club and make these soups the centerpiece of your winter meals!

The Winter Soups

Vegetable Soup – One of our favorites in the New Year chock full of all sorts of vegetables simmered in a tomato and herb laced vegetable broth. Very healthy and satisfying! Baked Potato with Bacon & Scallions – Local Potatoes, onions, garlic and vegetable stock are pureed with a touch of Pittsford Farms Dairy Cream and extra sharp cheddar from the Cuba Cheese Shoppe and delivered with Crispy Bacon and fresh scallions for topping. Matzoh Ball – A rich broth made from Autumn's Harvest chickens combined with sliced Remembrance Farms carrots, fluffy matzoh balls and fresh dill. Black Bean & Local Corn Chili – Callan Farms black beans are cooked slowly with aromatics and then combined with flash frozen corn from the Hudson Valley, canned summer tomatoes and vegetables and spices to created a richly flavored and filling chili.  Caldo Verde – A simple and tasty Portuguese stew comprised of local potatoes, onion, garlic and chorizo sausage simmered with kale and mustard greens. Roasted Vegetable Bisque with Coriander and Lemon – We roast cases of local eggplant, peppers and tomatoes at the end of Summer and freeze them just to enjoy this soup in the coldest days of winter! Combined with charred onions, garlic, and vegetable stock, seasoned with toasted cumin and coriander and pureed with a splash of lemon, this soup is a brightly flavored reminder of the warmer months to come. Pho - A Vietnamese favorite made with slowly simmered bone broth (Shirk's Meats) and aromatics and served with thinly sliced beef, rice noodles, Thai basil, bean sprouts and lime wedges. Stuffed Cabbage Soup - Old Ridge Farm Cabbage is simmered with diced tomato, carrots, onions, garlic and our rich chicken stock and finished with ground chicken, brown rice and a bit of fresh dill and parsley to create a spin on the classic Polish dish.

How it Works

Each week, Chef Lindsay Steinberg prepares a fresh soup for you to pick up or have delivered to your home or office. Each quart of soup is 4 cups and serves 2-3 people. The first soup delivery begins on Tuesday January 31st and continues every Tuesday for 8 weeks with the last delivery on March 21st. Delicious!

What it Costs

The all-inclusive cost of $105 includes eight (8) weeks of soups available for pick up at our Fairport location every Tuesday between 4:30 pm and 6:30 pm.  8 weeks of delivery is available for an additional $50, but delivery is limited to the first 12 customers to sign up. Add a green side salad (serves 2-3) with chef’s choice of dressing to your weekly delivery for $60. Add an additional quart of soup for $60. *Please inquire about our vegan and gluten free options for an additional cost.

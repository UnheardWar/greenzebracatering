Autumn Soup Club is Back!

This autumn, only the freshest, local ingredients from sustainable farms go into our autumn collection. Chef Lindsay Steinberg McGrail has created eight new seasonal recipes that focus on the local foods of autumn. Join the Green Zebra Autumn Soup Club and make these soups the centerpiece of your autumn meals!

The Autumn Soups

Chicken and Poblano Tortilla Soup – Lagoner Farms green chile and Fisher Hill chicken combine with fresh corn tortillas and veggies in a rich, spiced chicken broth to create a delicious meal. Includes tortilla strips, lime wedges and cilantro on the side to garnish. Cauliflower-Miso – A creamy bisque of LaMora Farms cauliflower, Fisher Hill leeks, and Juniper Hill carrots prepared with fresh vegetable stock, almond milk and white miso, giving it a healthy richness. French Onion – 3 kinds of local onions from Fledgling Crow Farms and broth made from Fisher Hill chickens are used in this lighter interpretation of one of Lindsay's favorite Julia Child recipes. Winter Squash and Ginger Congee – An Asian style local squash soup thats sure to warm you up on a chilly fall night!  Flavored with ginger, thai basil and cilantro and thickened with white rice, it is pure comfort in a bowl.  Served with crispy ginger slices and scallions on the side to garnish.  Minestrone – A hearty Italian favorite prepared with local vegetables, white and kidney beans and a tomato-parmesan broth. Local Corn and Mushroom Chowder – Sweet Hudson Valley corn and Smugtown mushrooms combine with potatoes, carrots, celery, and Pittsford Farms dairy whole milk to create a satisfying (but not heavy) chowder. Lemon Chicken, Spinach and Israeli Couscous - One of our favorite versions of chicken soup with the tang of fresh lemon, organic baby spinach, fresh dill and Israeli couscous to elevate it to meal status. Includes crumbled Bulgarian feta cheese to garnish. French Lentil Soup - A fall/winter staple with vegetables, hearty greens and finished with a touch of aged balsamic vinegar.

How it Works

Each week, Chef Lindsay Steinberg prepares a fresh autumn soup for you to pick up or have delivered to your home or office.  Each quart of soup is 4 cups and serves 2-3 people. The first soup delivery begins on November 1st and continues every Tuesday for 8 weeks ending on December 20. Delicious!

What it Costs

The all-inclusive cost of $105 includes eight (8) weeks of soups available for pick up at our Fairport location every Tuesday between 4:30 pm and 6:30 pm.  8 weeks of delivery is available for an additional $50, but delivery is limited to the first 12 customers to sign up. Add a green side salad (serves 2-3) with chef’s choice of dressing to your weekly delivery for $60. Add an additional quart of soup for $60. *Please inquire about our vegan and gluten free options for an additional cost.
